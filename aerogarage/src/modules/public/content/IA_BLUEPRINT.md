- Home (`/`)

## CTA Routing Map
### Primary Conversion CTAs
- Request Proposal -> Commercial lead form
- Request Service Proposal -> Service scoping form
- Apply for Training -> Training application funnel
- View Open Roles -> Careers listing
- Submit Inquiry -> Contact intake form

### Secondary CTAs
- Explore Services
- Download Company Profile
- View Program Modules
- Submit CV
- Call Corporate Office

### Tertiary CTAs
- Email Sales
- Email Careers
- Partner With AMC
- Book Technical Meeting
- Contact Recruitment

## Notes
- All page wireframes are implemented in code through `iaBlueprint.js` + `WireframePage.jsx`.
- Placeholders are intentionally structured for upcoming production copy pass.
- Training page route is public (`/training`), while portal routes remain (`/training/login`, `/training/dashboard`).
